Tomic Tutoring Services

Services

Recommended session length is 1.5 hours.

Location

Sessions can be conducted at a place of your choosing - your home or mine are both options.

Special Online Sessions

Also, there is the option of conducting special remote based tutoring taking advantage of whiteboard web services. This is done over the Internet and sessions can be done in blocks of 15 mintues.
